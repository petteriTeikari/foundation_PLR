## 6. Text Content

### Title
"The Dense Signal Club"

### Caption
"TSFM preprocessing concepts transfer across domains that share four properties: high sampling rate (>1 Hz), continuous underlying process, gaps representing measurement errors (not information), and correlated neighboring samples. PLR, ECG, vibration, seismic, and audio all belong to this 'club'. EHRs, business KPIs, and logistics data don'tâ€”they need domain-specific approaches like GMAN, M-GAM, or traditional forecasting methods."
